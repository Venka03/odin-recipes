Olivie recipe

Olivie is must-have on a new year table of millions of people from Ukraine. It is one of the best salad ever and it can be both vegan or not. This salad is easy to do, so it is a good option when you do not have a lot of time, but what to eat something delicious

And if you here, then you probably want to make it, so it is how to do olivie at home and what you need for it

Ingredients

  • 6 potatoes, peeled
  • 1 carrot, or more to taste
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 6 large pickles, cut into cubes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained
  • 1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise, or to taste

Steps to do

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add potatoes and carrot and return to a boil. Add whole eggs and cook until potatoes are tender, 20 to 30 minutes. Drain and slightly cool mixture.
  2. Chop potatoes and carrot. Peel and chop eggs.
  3. Mix potatoes, carrot, eggs, pickles, peas, ham, and parsley together in a large bowl; stir in mayonnaise until salad is evenly coated.

So this is how you can make olivie at home. Enjoy)
