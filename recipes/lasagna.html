Lasagna recipe

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Lasagna originated in Italy during the Middle Ages. But still it is considered to be one of the best italian dish which people love all over the world.

And if you here, then you probably want to make it, so it is how to do lasagna at home and what you need for it

Ingredients

  • 250 gr of corrugated sheets for lasagna Don Vittorio
  • 500 gr of complete red sauce Don Vittorio
  • 400 gr of ground meat
  • 200 gr of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 30 gr of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 20g butter
  • 50 gr flour without preparing Blanca Flor
  • 500 ml of milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg

Steps to do

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. In a saucepan add the butter and Blanca Flor flour. Add salt, pepper and nutmeg to taste
  3. Add the milk and move until the mixture is homogeneous, ensure that the sauce is liquid. Reserve the white sauce.
  4. Mix the complete Don Vittorio red sauce with the previously cooked ground meat.
  5. Place a first layer of white sauce and lasagna sheets in the dish. Don't overlap them.
  6. Continue the assembly in the following order: red sauce, white sauce, mozzarella cheese and lasagna sheets. Repeat the previous indication 4 times.
  7. Finish the assembly by covering with white sauce, mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese
  8. Bake for 10 minutes at 180°C. Let stand for 5 minutes and voila!

So this is how you can make lasagna at home. Enjoy)
